About Us

In the era of large corporations, the voice of the individual is being silenced. We, as the Anti-Establishment, aim to give the voice back to the people and allow them to be heard. In our system today, there are too many ways that corporations can take advantage of small communities. They use their money and power to be able to boost their profits at the expense of the people and our land.

This cannot be allowed anymore.

We refuse to idly sit by any longer. We aim to push our collective efforts to become true catalysts for change and refuse to stop till the day that corporate profits will stop taking precedence over human lives.
